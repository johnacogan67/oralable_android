# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

JAC Dental Solutions Limited ("Oralable", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the Oralable mobile application and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Sensor Data

When you use an Oralable device, we collect:

- Heart rate and blood oxygen measurements
- Accelerometer data (movement patterns)
- Bruxism (teeth grinding) detection events
- Sleep position and activity levels

### Account Information

When you create an account using Sign in with Apple, we receive:

- Your Apple ID identifier (anonymized if you choose)
- Your email address (or Apple's relay email if hidden)
- Your display name (optional)

### HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, we may read and write:

- Heart rate data
- Sleep analysis data
- Blood oxygen saturation

### Usage Data

We automatically collect:

- App usage patterns and feature interactions
- Device type and operating system version
- App performance and crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide real-time health monitoring and bruxism detection
- Display your health metrics and trends
- Enable data sharing with your dental professional (with your consent)
- Improve our services and develop new features
- Send important service notifications
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

### With Your Dental Professional

You may choose to share your health data with a dental professional by generating a share code. When you share data:

- Your dentist can view your bruxism events and health metrics
- You control which data is shared and can revoke access at any time
- Shared data is transmitted securely via Apple CloudKit

### With Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- Apple (for CloudKit storage and Sign in with Apple)
- Analytics providers (anonymized usage data only)
- Legal authorities when required by law

## HealthKit Data

We treat HealthKit data with special care:

- HealthKit data is only accessed with your explicit permission
- We never share HealthKit data with third parties for advertising
- HealthKit data is stored securely on your device and in iCloud
- You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- All data transmission uses TLS encryption
- Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard methods
- Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel
- We conduct regular security assessments

## Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while your account is active
- Sensor data is retained for 12 months unless you request deletion
- After account deletion, we remove personal data within 30 days
- Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics

## Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

### Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

### Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate personal data.

### Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

### Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

### Object

Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes.

### Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission or your local supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@oralable.com.

## Children's Privacy

Oralable is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with GDPR.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

- Posting the updated policy in the app
- Sending a notification to your registered email
- Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
