# Support Center

Welcome to Oralable Support. Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most from your Oralable device.

## Getting Started

### What You Need

- An Oralable sensor device or compatible ANR MuscleSense device
- iPhone or iPad with iOS 17.0 or later
- Bluetooth enabled on your device
- An Apple ID for Sign in with Apple

### Setting Up Your Account

1. Download Oralable from the App Store
2. Open the app and tap "Sign in with Apple"
3. Choose whether to share or hide your email
4. Complete the brief onboarding process

### Pairing Your Device

1. Ensure your Oralable device is charged
2. Open the Oralable app
3. Navigate to the Devices tab
4. Tap "Connect Device"
5. Select your Oralable device from the list
6. Wait for the connection to complete

## How to Connect Your Device

### First-Time Connection

When connecting your device for the first time:

1. Make sure Bluetooth is enabled in your iPhone or iPad Settings
2. Ensure your Oralable device is powered on and in pairing mode
3. In the app, tap the Devices tab
4. Tap "Scan for Devices"
5. Your device will appear in the list once detected
6. Tap your device name to connect
7. The connection indicator will turn green when successful

### Reconnecting

Your Oralable device will automatically reconnect when:

- You open the app and the device is nearby
- The device comes into Bluetooth range

If automatic reconnection fails, manually connect from the Devices tab.

## Troubleshooting

### Device Not Found During Scan

If your device does not appear when scanning:

- Ensure the device is powered on and charged
- Move the device closer to your iPhone or iPad
- Check that Bluetooth is enabled in iOS Settings
- Close and reopen the Oralable app
- Restart your iPhone or iPad
- If using an ANR MuscleSense, ensure it is in pairing mode

### Connection Drops Frequently

If your connection is unstable:

- Keep your phone within 10 meters of the device
- Reduce interference from other Bluetooth devices
- Ensure the device battery is sufficiently charged
- Update to the latest version of the app
- Check for iOS updates

### Data Not Syncing

If your health data is not appearing:

- Verify the device is connected (green indicator)
- Wait a few moments for initial data to arrive
- Check that the device is positioned correctly
- Close and reopen the app
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the device

### Heart Rate Shows Zero or Incorrect Values

If heart rate readings seem wrong:

- Ensure proper skin contact with the sensor
- Clean the sensor surface
- Adjust device positioning
- Wait 30 seconds for the reading to stabilize
- Remove any barriers between the sensor and skin

### HealthKit Data Not Appearing

If data is not syncing to Apple Health:

- Open iOS Settings and navigate to Health
- Tap Data Access and Devices
- Find Oralable and ensure permissions are enabled
- Grant access to the specific data types you want to sync

### App Crashes or Freezes

If the app is not responding:

- Force close the app and reopen it
- Restart your iPhone or iPad
- Update to the latest version of Oralable
- If issues persist, delete and reinstall the app

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Oralable a medical device?

No. Oralable is a wellness device intended for informational purposes only. It is not designed to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.

### How accurate is the bruxism detection?

Oralable uses accelerometer data and proprietary algorithms to detect teeth grinding patterns. While our technology is designed to identify bruxism events, accuracy may vary based on device positioning, sleep position, and individual differences. Use the data as a general indicator and discuss patterns with your dentist.

### Can I share my data with my dentist?

Yes. You can share your health data with a dental professional by generating a share code in the app. Your dentist enters this code in the Oralable for Dentists app to view your data. You can revoke access at any time.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap Subscriptions
4. Select Oralable
5. Tap Cancel Subscription

### Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage. Your data is stored securely in Apple CloudKit. We do not sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### What devices are compatible?

Oralable works with:

- Oralable sensor devices
- ANR MuscleSense devices

The app requires an iPhone or iPad running iOS 17.0 or later with Bluetooth 4.0 support.

### How long is data retained?

Sensor data is retained for 12 months by default. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support.

### Can I use Oralable without a subscription?

Yes. The free tier includes real-time monitoring and basic analytics. Premium subscriptions unlock additional features such as advanced analytics, extended history, and unlimited data export.

### Does Oralable work in the background?

Oralable can maintain a Bluetooth connection in the background for continuous monitoring. Enable Background App Refresh in iOS Settings for best results.

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data:

1. Open the Oralable app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap Account
4. Select "Delete Account"
5. Confirm your choice

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
