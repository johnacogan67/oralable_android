Why We Started

Bruxism is surprisingly common, yet surprisingly hard to monitor. Traditional devices cost €600-800, require clinical setup, and are uncomfortable enough that most people stop using them.

We believed there had to be a better way. What if monitoring could be affordable, comfortable, and something you'd actually use every night?

That question led us to PPG technology—the same light-based sensing used in smartwatches—applied in a new way to detect jaw muscle activity. After years of development and testing, we're bringing this technology to market.

JAC Dental Solutions Limited was founded in Dublin, Ireland, with a simple mission: make bruxism monitoring accessible to everyone who needs it.

Different by Design

Comfort First

We chose PPG over EMG specifically because it enables a smaller, more comfortable device. A device you don't wear doesn't collect any data.

Building in Public

We believe in transparency. We share our progress, listen to feedback, and iterate openly with our community of early adopters.

Starting Simple

We're launching as a wellness device first. This lets us get the product into users' hands faster while we build the evidence for medical certification.

What We're Not (Yet)

We believe in being upfront about where we are in our journey.

Not a Medical Device

Oralable is a wellness tool. It doesn't diagnose or treat bruxism—your dentist does that. We provide data to support better conversations with your healthcare provider.

A Small Team

We're not a big corporation with 24/7 support centers. Response times may vary, but we read every message and genuinely care about your experience.

Learning and Improving

This is an early product. We're learning with every user and improving with every release. Your patience and feedback make us better.

Protected Innovation

Our technology is protected by granted patents in the United States and European Union.

We've developed a novel approach to intraoral PPG-based muscle activity detection that enables comfortable, continuous bruxism monitoring.
