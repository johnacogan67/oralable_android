Setting Up Your Oralable

1. Download the App

  • Search "Oralable" on the App Store
  • Requires iOS 16 or later
  • iPhone only (iPad support coming soon)

2. Sign In

  • Open the Oralable app
  • Tap "Sign in with Apple"
  • Your data stays private with Apple's secure authentication

3. Charge Your Device

  • Place the sensor in the magnetic charging case
  • The LED will indicate charging status
  • A full charge takes approximately 2 hours
  • Full charge provides 8-10 hours of monitoring

4. Connect Your Device

  • Enable Bluetooth on your iPhone
  • Open the Oralable app
  • Go to the Devices tab
  • Press the button on the sensor to wake it
  • Tap to connect when the device appears

5. Your First Recording

  • Apply a fresh adhesive to the sensor
  • Attach the sensor to your cheek (over the masseter muscle)
  • Tap "Start Recording" in the app
  • Wear during sleep
  • In the morning, review your results in the app

Troubleshooting

Device Won't Connect

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your iPhone
  • Check the device is charged (press the button, LED should light)
  • Move within 2 meters of your iPhone
  • Try turning the device off and on by holding the button
  • Close and reopen the Oralable app
  • Restart your iPhone if the issue persists
  • Try "Forget Device" in iPhone Bluetooth settings, then reconnect

No Data Showing

  • Ensure the device shows "Ready" or "Connected" status in the app
  • Check the sensor is properly positioned on your cheek
  • Wait 10-15 seconds for data to start appearing
  • Check the device battery level isn't critically low
  • Ensure good skin contact with the sensor

Data Not Syncing

  • Check you have an internet connection
  • Pull down to refresh in the app
  • Ensure iCloud is enabled for Oralable (Settings > Your Name > iCloud)
  • Check your iCloud storage isn't full
  • Sign out and back in to your Apple ID if issues persist

App Crashes

  • Update to the latest app version from the App Store
  • Restart your iPhone
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your data is backed up to iCloud)
  • Contact support with details about when the crash occurs

Poor Signal Quality

  • Reposition the sensor on your cheek
  • Clean the sensor surface gently with a soft, dry cloth
  • Ensure good skin contact—the sensor should sit flat
  • Try a fresh adhesive if the current one is worn
  • Make sure the sensor window is clean and unobstructed

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Oralable a medical device?

No. Oralable is a wellness monitoring tool designed to help you track jaw muscle activity patterns. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition. Always consult your dentist or healthcare provider for medical advice about bruxism.

How long does the battery last?

The Oralable sensor provides 8-10 hours of continuous monitoring on a full charge, which is enough for a full night's sleep plus margin.

Is it safe to sleep with?

Yes. The device uses medical-grade silicone and low-power Bluetooth. It's designed specifically for comfortable overnight wear. The sensor is small, lightweight, and adheres securely to your cheek.

Can I wear it while eating?

We recommend removing the device while eating or drinking for hygiene and comfort. The sensor is designed for sleep monitoring.

How do I clean the device?

Wipe the sensor with a soft, slightly damp cloth. Do not submerge in water—the device is splash resistant but not waterproof. Do not use harsh chemicals, alcohol, or abrasive materials.

How do I share data with my dentist?

Go to Settings → Share with Dentist → Generate Code. Give the 6-digit code to your dentist. They'll enter it in their Oralable for Dentists app to view your data. You can revoke access anytime.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings → Export Data to download your recording history in a standard format.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Go to iPhone Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Oralable → Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

What iPhone do I need?

Oralable requires an iPhone running iOS 16 or later with Bluetooth 5.0 support. This includes iPhone 8 and newer models.

When will Android be available?

Android support is planned for 2026. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when it's available.

Do you ship internationally?

Initially, we're shipping within Ireland and the European Union. Wider international availability is planned for later in 2026.

How often should I replace the adhesives?

We recommend using a fresh adhesive each night for optimal sensor contact and hygiene. A 30-day supply is included with your device.

What if I have sensitive skin?

Our adhesives are medical-grade and designed for sensitive skin. However, if you experience any irritation, discontinue use and contact your doctor. Do not use the device on broken or irritated skin.
