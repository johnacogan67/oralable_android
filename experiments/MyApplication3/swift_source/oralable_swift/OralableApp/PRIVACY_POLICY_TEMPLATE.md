## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

**On Your Device:**
- Recording sessions are stored locally on your iPhone/iPad
- Local storage is encrypted by iOS

**In CloudKit:**
- Health data shared with dentists is stored in Apple's CloudKit
- Uses iCloud.com.jacdental.oralable.shared container
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Stored in Apple's data centers (location varies by region)

**In HealthKit:**
- Data written to HealthKit is stored in Apple's Health app
- Controlled by iOS security and encryption
- We cannot access this data without your permission

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure authentication using Sign in with Apple
- Encrypted local storage
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure coding practices

**However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.** While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 4.3 Data Retention

**Active Accounts:**
- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Dentist-shared data persists until you revoke access

**Deleted Accounts:**
- When you delete your account, we delete your personal information within 30 days
- Some information may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes
- Dentists who previously had access retain their copy of historical data you shared

**Recording Sessions:**
- Stored on your device indefinitely unless you delete them
- You can delete individual sessions at any time
