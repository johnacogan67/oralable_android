Frequently Asked Questions

Is Oralable a medical device?

No. Oralable is a consumer wellness device for personal awareness of muscle activity patterns. It is NOT a medical device and does NOT diagnose, treat, or cure any medical condition. It is similar to a fitness tracker — useful for personal awareness but not for medical purposes. If you have health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare provider.

What devices are compatible with Oralable?

Oralable works with the Oralable sensor device and ANR MuscleSe√±se devices. Both connect via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) 4.0 or later. Your iPhone or iPad must be running iOS 18.0 or later.

How do I connect my device?

Open the Oralable app and tap "Connect" on the dashboard. The app will scan for nearby devices. When your Oralable sensor appears, tap it to connect. The status will show "Ready" when connected.

How do I record a session?

Once connected to your device, tap the record button on the dashboard. The app will record your muscle activity until you tap stop. You can review recorded sessions later.

How do I export my data?

Go to the Export tab in the app and tap "Export Data as CSV". You can then share the CSV file via email, AirDrop, or save it to Files.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. If you choose to share data, it's encrypted and transmitted via Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure. We never sell your data or share it without your explicit consent. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting

Device Won't Connect

  1. Check Bluetooth is enabled - Go to iPhone Settings > Bluetooth and ensure it's turned on
  2. Restart the Oralable device - Turn it off and on again, or place it on the charger briefly
  3. Restart the app - Force close Oralable and reopen it
  4. Restart your iPhone - Sometimes a full restart resolves Bluetooth issues
  5. Check battery - Ensure your Oralable device is charged

Data Not Appearing on Dashboard

  1. Verify connection - Check that the device shows "Ready" (green status)
  2. Wait for data - It may take a few seconds after connection for data to appear
  3. Check device placement - Ensure the sensor is making good contact

App Crashes or Freezes

  1. Update the app - Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  2. Restart the app - Force close and reopen
  3. Restart your device - A fresh start often resolves issues
  4. Reinstall - Delete and reinstall the app
