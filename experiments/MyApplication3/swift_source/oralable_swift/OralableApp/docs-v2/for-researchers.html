Oralable for Research

Muscle activity data for academic and independent research.

Research Applications

Oralable provides researchers with an accessible way to collect muscle activity data. The device and companion app make it easy to gather data from research participants in various settings.

Academic Studies

Collect muscle activity data for behavioral research, sleep studies, or stress-related investigations.

Longitudinal Tracking

Participants can record sessions over time and export data for your analysis.

Independent Research

Affordable hardware and free data export make independent research feasible.

Comparative Studies

Support for ANR MuscleSeñse devices allows comparison with established research hardware.

Data Collection Features

  • CSV Export: Participants can export their data in standard CSV format
  • Timestamped Data: All recordings include precise timestamps
  • Raw Sensor Values: Access to underlying sensor measurements
  • Session Recording: Participants can record specific sessions for analysis
  • Self-Managed: Participants control their own data and sharing

Participant Privacy

Oralable is designed with privacy in mind. Participants maintain control of their own data and can export it directly to researchers. No centralized data collection occurs without participant action.

This approach simplifies IRB/ethics approval as participants manage their own data sharing.

Oralable for Researchers App

The companion app for researchers (coming Q2 2026) will allow:

  • Receive data shared by research participants
  • View aggregated patterns across participant groups
  • Export combined datasets for analysis
  • Track participant engagement

Hardware Compatibility

The Oralable app works with:

  • Oralable Device: Our consumer-grade muscle activity sensor
  • ANR MuscleSeñse: Research-grade EMG hardware for comparative studies
