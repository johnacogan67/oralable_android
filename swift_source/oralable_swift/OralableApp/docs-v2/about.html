What We're Building

Oralable is a personal wellness tool that provides biofeedback about jaw muscle activity. It's designed for people curious about their patterns — whether during sleep, work, or daily activities.

What Oralable Is

  • A consumer wellness device
  • A biofeedback tool for personal awareness
  • Similar in concept to fitness trackers
  • Accessible and affordable
  • Privacy-focused (you control your data)

What We're Not (Yet)

Oralable is currently a wellness device, not a medical device. This means:

  • We do NOT diagnose any medical conditions
  • We do NOT provide medical advice
  • We are NOT approved by FDA, CE, or other medical regulatory bodies
  • We are NOT a substitute for professional healthcare

Future Plans: We are exploring a medical device pathway for future versions. This would involve clinical validation studies, regulatory approvals, and partnerships with healthcare institutions. However, this is a multi-year undertaking and our current product should be understood as a wellness/biofeedback tool only.
