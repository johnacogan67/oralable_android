Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

JAC Dental Solutions Limited ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Oralable mobile application and Oralable for Researchers mobile application (collectively, the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Apps.

Our Commitment: Your biofeedback data belongs to you. We use industry-standard encryption, secure Apple infrastructure, and give you full control over who sees your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Sensor Data

When you use an Oralable device, we collect:

  • PPG (Photoplethysmography) Data: Light-based sensor readings used to monitor muscle activity
  • Accelerometer Data: Movement and orientation data (X, Y, Z axes) used to detect activity patterns
  • Muscle Activity Data: Calculated metrics derived from sensor readings

1.2 Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • A unique user identifier (not your Apple ID)
  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (if you choose to share it, or Apple's private relay email)

We do not receive or store your Apple ID password.

1.3 Device Information

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Bluetooth device identifiers for paired Oralable devices

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Biofeedback Display: Display real-time and recorded muscle activity data
  • Session Recording: Store your recorded sessions for later review
  • Data Export: Enable you to export your data as CSV

2.2 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Share your data with anyone without your explicit consent
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking services

3. Data Sharing

3.1 How Sharing Works

If you choose to share your data with a researcher:

  • You generate a 6-digit share code in the Oralable app
  • The researcher enters this code in Oralable for Researchers
  • A secure connection is established via Apple CloudKit
  • Share codes expire after 48 hours for security

3.2 Revoking Access

You can revoke any shared access to your data at any time through the app. Once revoked, the recipient will no longer receive updates and their cached data will be removed.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Local Device: Session data is stored locally on your iPhone/iPad
  • Apple CloudKit: If you choose to share data, it is stored securely in Apple's iCloud infrastructure

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • CloudKit data is encrypted at rest using Apple's security infrastructure
  • We use Apple's Sign in with Apple for secure authentication
  • No passwords are stored in our systems

5. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until you delete the app or clear data in settings
  • CloudKit Data: Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account deletion
  • Shared Data: Retained while sharing relationship is active; removed when you revoke access

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You can export all your data from the app at any time using the export feature.

6.2 Right to Rectification

Contact us to correct any inaccurate personal information.

6.3 Right to Erasure

You can delete your account and all associated data through the app or by contacting us.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

Export your data in standard CSV format at any time.

6.5 Right to Object

You can opt out of data sharing at any time by revoking access.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: For data sharing and optional features
  • Contract: To provide the biofeedback services you've requested
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and ensure security
