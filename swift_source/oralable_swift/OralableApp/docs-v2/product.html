Muscle Activity Monitor

WELLNESS DEVICE

Oralable is a muscle activity monitor for personal awareness. It is NOT a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.

Oralable is a wearable biofeedback device that monitors muscle activity in your jaw. Think of it like a fitness tracker, but for your facial muscles.

How It Works

The Oralable sensor uses photoplethysmography (PPG) technology to detect muscle activity. The sensor attaches comfortably and connects to your iPhone via Bluetooth.

Wear

Attach the lightweight sensor to monitor muscle activity

Connect

Pair with the Oralable iOS app via Bluetooth

View

See real-time muscle activity on your dashboard

Record

Save sessions and export data for personal analysis

Like a Fitness Tracker for Your Jaw

Just as a fitness tracker shows you steps and heart rate for personal awareness, Oralable shows you jaw muscle activity. It's biofeedback, not medical monitoring.

  • Fitness Tracker: Steps, heart rate, sleep → Personal wellness awareness
  • Oralable: Jaw muscle activity → Personal awareness of clenching/grinding patterns

App Features

  • Real-time display: See muscle activity as it happens
  • Session recording: Record and review activity sessions
  • Data export: Export your data as CSV
  • Simple interface: Clean, easy-to-understand display
