Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

Introduction

JAC Dental Solutions Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Oralable application and sensor device.

We are registered in Ireland and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account using Sign in with Apple, we may receive:

  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (or Apple's private relay email)

Sensor Data

When you use the Oralable sensor, we collect:

  • PPG (photoplethysmography) readings
  • Accelerometer data
  • Temperature readings
  • Session timestamps and duration

Health Metrics

We derive the following from sensor data:

  • Muscle activity patterns
  • Grinding/clenching episode detection
  • Sleep session summaries
  • Historical trends and patterns

HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, we may access:

  • Heart rate data
  • Sleep analysis data

Device Information

We automatically collect:

  • iPhone model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Sensor device identifier

Usage Information

We collect anonymous usage data including:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Session duration
  • App performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the bruxism monitoring service
  • Display insights and trends in the app
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Share data with your dentist (only with your explicit consent)
  • Improve our products and services
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service notifications

Data Sharing

Your Dentist

You may choose to share your data with a dental professional by generating a sharing code. This is entirely optional and under your control. You can revoke access at any time.

Apple (iCloud)

Your data is synced via Apple's CloudKit service and stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and controlled by you.

We Never

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing without your consent
  • Store your health data on our servers (it remains on your device and iCloud)

HealthKit Data

HealthKit data is handled with special care:

  • Access requires your explicit permission
  • Data is used strictly for health insights within the app
  • We never share HealthKit data with third parties
  • We never use HealthKit data for advertising
  • You control what we can access in iOS Settings

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Apple CloudKit infrastructure with built-in encryption
  • Secure BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) communication with the sensor
  • No storage of health data on our servers

Your health data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have direct access to it.

Data Retention

  • Active account: Your data is retained while your account is active
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all associated data is removed within 30 days
  • Request deletion: Email privacy@oralable.com to request data deletion

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@oralable.com.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses:

  • Minimal, privacy-respecting analytics
  • Essential cookies for form submission
  • No advertising cookies
  • No third-party trackers

Children's Privacy

Oralable is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data is processed within the European Union through Apple's Dublin data center. We do not transfer your personal data outside the EU/EEA except where adequate protections are in place as required by GDPR.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification
  • In-app notification
  • Updated "Effective Date" at the top of this policy

Continued use of Oralable after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
