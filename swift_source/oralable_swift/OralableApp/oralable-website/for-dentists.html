The Problem

Your patients report subjectively. "I think I grind sometimes." "My partner says I clench." By the time you see wear patterns or tooth damage, it's often too late for prevention.

The Solution

Continuous monitoring data shared directly to your app. See patterns over weeks and months. Make treatment decisions based on objective measurements, not guesswork.

Dentist App Screenshot

How It Works

1

Patient Downloads App

Your patient gets Oralable from the App Store and starts monitoring at home.

2

Share a Code

The patient generates a 6-digit code and shares it with you during their visit.

3

View Their Data

Access their monitoring data in the Oralable for Dentists app. See trends and patterns.

4

Track Over Time

Monitor treatment effectiveness. See if night guards are working. Adjust care accordingly.

Benefits for Your Practice

  • 📈

    Objective Measurement

    Move beyond patient self-reporting. Get real data about grinding frequency, intensity, and patterns.

  • 📋

    Treatment Monitoring

    Track night guard effectiveness over time. See if interventions are working before the next appointment.

  • 🤝

    Patient Engagement

    Patients who see their own data stay motivated. They understand why treatment matters.

  • ⏱

    Efficient Consultations

    Review data before appointments. Spend chair time on care, not history-taking.
