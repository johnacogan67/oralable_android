What is PPG?

PPG (photoplethysmography) uses light to detect blood flow changes in tissue. Our sensor applies this technology to detect jaw muscle activity, providing a comfortable alternative to traditional EMG-based monitoring.

The Oralable sensor uses a multi-LED configuration to accurately detect when your masseter muscle is active, indicating clenching or grinding events.
