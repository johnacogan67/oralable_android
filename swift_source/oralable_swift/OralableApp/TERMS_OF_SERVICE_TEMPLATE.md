## 2. Description of Services

### 2.1 Oralable (Patient App)

Oralable is a wellness and health monitoring application that:
- Connects to Oralable sensor devices via Bluetooth
- Collects and displays physiological data (bruxism events, heart rate, SpO2, temperature, movement)
- Stores recording sessions locally and in CloudKit
- Enables data sharing with dental professionals
- Integrates with Apple HealthKit
- Provides analytics and insights

### 2.2 Oralable for Dentists

Oralable for Dentists is a professional monitoring application that:
- Receives patient data shared via share codes
- Displays patient bruxism patterns and analytics
- Provides multi-patient management
- Enables data export for personal records
- Offers practice-level insights

### 2.3 Not a Medical Device

**IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:**

**ORALABLE IS NOT A MEDICAL DEVICE.** It is intended for:
