2. Description of Services

2.1 Oralable (Patient App)

Oralable is a wellness application that:

  • Connects to Oralable sensor devices via Bluetooth
  • Displays health metrics including heart rate, blood oxygen, and movement data
  • Detects and tracks bruxism (teeth grinding) events
  • Enables optional data sharing with dental professionals
  • Integrates with Apple HealthKit

2.2 Oralable for Dentists

Oralable for Dentists enables dental professionals to:

  • Receive patient health data shared through the patient app
  • View patient bruxism patterns and health metrics
  • Track patient progress over time
  • Export data for patient records
