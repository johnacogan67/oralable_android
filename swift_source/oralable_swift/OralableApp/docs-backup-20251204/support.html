Getting Started

For Patients (Oralable App)

1
Download the App
Get Oralable from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
2
Sign In
Use Sign in with Apple for secure, private authentication.
3
Pair Your Device
Go to the Devices tab and tap "Scan" to find your Oralable sensor. Tap to connect.
4
Start Monitoring
Once connected, you'll see real-time data on your dashboard.
5
Share with Your Dentist (Optional)
Go to Settings > Share with Dentist > Generate Code. Give this code to your dentist.

For Dentists (Oralable for Dentists App)

1
Download Oralable for Dentists
Search for "Oralable for Dentists" in the App Store.
2
Sign In
Use Sign in with Apple for your professional account.
3
Add a Patient
Tap "Add Patient" and enter the 6-digit code your patient provides.
4
View Patient Data
Once connected, you'll automatically receive patient health data.

Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are compatible with Oralable?

Oralable works with the Oralable sensor device and ANR MuscleSe√±se devices. Both connect via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) 4.0 or later. Your iPhone or iPad must be running iOS 17.0 or later.

Where can I buy an Oralable device?

Oralable devices are available on Tindie. Visit tindie.com and search for "Oralable" to find our official store.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure and Sign in with Apple. We never sell your data or share it without your explicit consent. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I share my data with my dentist?

In the Oralable app, go to Settings > Share with Dentist > Generate Share Code. A 6-digit code will be displayed that's valid for 48 hours. Give this code to your dentist to enter in their Oralable for Dentists app.

Can I revoke my dentist's access to my data?

Yes, at any time. Go to Settings > Shared Connections, select the dentist, and tap "Revoke Access". They will immediately lose access to your data.

What does the Premium subscription include?

Premium unlocks: unlimited dentist connections, advanced analytics and insights, unlimited data export, historical data analysis, custom alerts, and priority support. Free tier includes full real-time monitoring, basic analytics, and 1 dentist connection.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > Oralable, then tap "Cancel Subscription". You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Does Oralable work with Apple Health?

Yes! With your permission, Oralable can save heart rate, blood oxygen, and sleep data to Apple Health. Enable this in Settings > HealthKit Integration.

Troubleshooting

Device Won't Connect via Bluetooth

Try these steps in order:

  1. Check Bluetooth is enabled - Go to iPhone Settings > Bluetooth and ensure it's turned on
  2. Restart the Oralable device - Turn it off and on again, or place it on the charger briefly
  3. Restart the app - Force close Oralable and reopen it
  4. Forget and re-pair - In iPhone Settings > Bluetooth, find the Oralable device, tap the (i) icon, and select "Forget This Device". Then scan again in the app
  5. Restart your iPhone - Sometimes a full restart resolves Bluetooth issues
  6. Check battery - Ensure your Oralable device is charged (LED should indicate battery status)

Data Not Appearing on Dashboard

  1. Verify connection - Check that the device shows "Ready" (green) in the Devices tab
  2. Wait for data - It may take 10-15 seconds after connection for data to appear
  3. Check device placement - Ensure the sensor is making good contact with skin
  4. Check for interference - Move away from other Bluetooth devices temporarily

Share Code Not Working

  1. Check expiration - Share codes expire after 48 hours. Generate a new one if needed
  2. Verify the code - Ensure all 6 digits are entered correctly
  3. Check internet connection - Both patient and dentist need internet access
  4. Try again later - CloudKit may be temporarily unavailable. Wait a few minutes and try again

App Crashes or Freezes

  1. Update the app - Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  2. Restart the app - Force close and reopen
  3. Restart your device - A fresh start often resolves issues
  4. Reinstall - Delete and reinstall the app (your data is backed up to CloudKit)
  5. Contact support - If issues persist, email us with details about what happened
